Women Cell

The empowered in their capacities develop confidence!!

The health and safety concerns of women are paramount for the well being of a country. The institute has established a Women Cell in the college campus to empower female students and staff members in order to develop confidence, to enhance understanding of issues related to women and to make the college campus a safe place for female students and staffs.

AIM

To commemorate the achievements of women, to acknowledge the special status they deserve in society and to give protection for female gender.

OBJECTIVES OF THE CELL

  • To offer awareness programmes/ Guest Lectures and Workshop for female gender to empower and advance their development as individuals in their own right.
  • To create a conducive counseling environment for female gender to share their problems.
  • Observing the law on Sexual Harassment
