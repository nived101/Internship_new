Counselling Services

Manodarpan

An initiative by the Ministry of Education ( formerly) Human Resource Development, Government of India as part of Atma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyan.
This portal is to provide Psychosocial Support for Mental Health & Well Being of Students during the COVID Outbreak and beyond.
Further details can be obtained at: https://manodarpan.education.gov.in/
National Toll-Free Helpline Number: 844 844 0632 (to address students' psychosocial issues)


National Tele-Mental Health Programme of India

Your Dost

Students of NIT Puducherry can register with Your DOST through Institute internet services. Thereafter, the student can avail 24x7 services irrespective of his location using same device. All conversations and counseling details will be confidential with the student and the counselor only.
Further details can be obtained at: https://yourdost.com
For any issues please reach out to us on:
Email : customersupport&yourdost.com
Phone : 08047097654
