Placement Procedure


Why Recruit at NITPY

The purpose of an institution of higher learning is to impart/inculcate the best of the academic experience in a chosen/specific/specialization whose pyramidal base, firmly rests on the strong core curriculum in the initial two years coupled with the specialized knowledge in the final two years. From day one, faculty in particular and all other staff emphasize on an all-round growth/development of the student with focus on academics. As a consequence, the interpersonal and career skills (both oral and written) apart from the other required technical skills, are honed resulting in professionally well grown technocrats, ready to face the challenges thereof are available to recruit and hence NIT Puducherry stands among one of the best talent pools in the country.


Procedure
  • To recruit students for employment and internships, Corporate Firms and R&D organizations may fill up the JOB APPLICATION FORM and mail the same to placement@nitpy.ac.in or nitpyplacementcell@gmail.com

  • The filled in JAF is circulated to the students to keep them aware of the JOB PROFILE and other details about the company.

  • With mutual consent of Company and T & P cell, a date will be allotted to the Company. (In case of OFF Campus the company can mail the details such as where and when the recruitment drive will be conducted, job profile to madappa@nitpy.ac.in or placement@nitpy.ac.in which will be shared among students and interested students will attend the same)

  • Companies are expected to visit the campus on the date allotted to them and conduct the selection process.

  • After the selection process, the final list of students selected should be furnished to the TPO.
